Hands of clay

Welcome to the website of Natalie Valiente. Here you will find some examples of my work and information on classes. I run regular classes for able and disabled students. One of my most popular is the disabled pinchpot class.

All of the work on this page was created without a pottery wheel. It just goes to show how much can be done with the right techniques. Pottery wheels and many other tools are also available at the studio for all to use.

You can find more info via the links or by contacting me directly.
